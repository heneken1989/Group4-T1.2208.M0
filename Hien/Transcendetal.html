  • What is Transcendental Meditation?

    Also known as TM, Transcendental Meditation is a technique that requires you to silently chant a mantra twice for 20 minutes in a day. It is an efficient and effortless way to slowly bring your mind to its most silent being. This ultimate state is known as full consciousness or restful alertness.
    Mantra is a small Sanskrit sound that is different for each individual and can be given only by a certified TM teacher or Guru. Across the world, there are many certified teachers who teach this meditation and give mantra to individuals. Once you get your mantra, you can practice every day on your own.

  • ​How to Do Transcendental Meditation


    ...

    Take out 20 minutes right after waking up and 20 minutes right before going to bed for Transcendental Meditation.

    1.Sit comfortably on a chair, a bed, or wherever you like.
    2.Close your eyes and silently read the mantra inside of you for about 20 minutes.
    3.Breathe normally.
    4.If your mind wanders off, bring it back to the mantra.
    5.Once you are done chanting your mantra, give yourself a little pause for 2 minutes before opening your eyes.
    6.Repeat this process daily.

    TM technique is natural and you don’t have to believe in anything before practicing it. In fact, you can start practicing with some doubts and suspicions in your mind too. Once you spend a few days trying out the technique, you will automatically get your answers. There is no philosophy behind it. It also doesn’t require you to change your lifestyle.
